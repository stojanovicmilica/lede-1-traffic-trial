2025 on the Road in Serbia

How did the last year look like from the point of traffic accidents in Serbia.

By Milica Stojanović

Serbian police registered 33414 traffic accidents in the country during 2025. This analysis is a journalistic attempt to understand the offered data, so we can better understand traffic practises and behaviour on the road.

Police data are structured in couple of categories, but, as it will be seen later, their categories include only one option, meaning that we cannot know if there were some accident in which multiple things happened.

For example, out of 33414 accidents in total, police recorded 463 death cases, 13,133 injuries and 19,819 cases of material damage. It is not clear from their data how they categorised events in which, for example, both injuries and material damage happened.

For purposes of this story, we will treat every accident as an event in which only one of those things happened.

According to police data, people in Serbia in 2025 most often died in traffic accidents that included only one vehicle

Serbian capital, Belgrade, was, as expected, place of the most traffic accidents, 14,292 of them.

When it comes to distribution of type of the accident per city, it can be seen that accidents which include only one vehicle are the most common type of issues in the small Serbian cities.
